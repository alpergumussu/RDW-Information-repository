# Connected Vehicle Landscape — Cybersecurity Reference Site

> Exploring the evolution of connected vehicles — from modern attack surfaces to regulatory frameworks and the ethical dimensions of automotive cybersecurity.

---

## About

This site was developed as part of a cybersecurity project for **RDW** (Rijksdienst voor het Wegverkeer), conducted by students of the HBO-ICT programme at **Windesheim University of Applied Sciences**.

The website and contents were created by Gerben Brouwer and Alper Gumussu

It covers the broader context surrounding modern automotive cybersecurity: how vehicles became networked attack surfaces, how industry and regulators have responded, and what ethical considerations arise at the intersection of mobility, connectivity, and security.
