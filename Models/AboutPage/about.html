About this masterclass

Target Audience

This masterclass is designed for anyone who wants to build a meaningful understanding of automotive cybersecurity. While some modules — particularly at higher DOK levels — touch on concepts that benefit from prior technical or regulatory exposure, no prior expertise is required to get started.

Where deeper knowledge becomes relevant, we provide the tools, definitions, and references needed to engage with the material and explore further where necessary. Content is structured using Webb's Depth of Knowledge (DOK) framework, which classifies material by the cognitive complexity of the task rather than the background of the individual reader.

Acknowledgements

Parts of the content presented in this masterclass build on the work of a previous project group, whose original materials can be found at masterclass.starryhill.nl. Their work serves as the foundation for this iteration, and all content drawn from it is properly referenced throughout this website to ensure transparency and authenticity.

Rather than duplicating what has already been done, this project takes their contribution as a starting point and builds further. We have reviewed the existing masterclass critically, identified gaps in scope, depth, and currency, and expanded the material to address those areas. The aim is to preserve the strengths of the original while extending it with updated insights, new perspectives, and additional resources that reflect the current state of the topic.
