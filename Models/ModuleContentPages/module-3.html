Q-01

What is an ECU, and approximately how many does a modern vehicle contain?

flip for answer

Answer

An Electronic Control Unit is a small, embedded computer that controls a specific function in the vehicle. A modern vehicle contains between 70 and 150 of them.

Q-02

Name three wireless communication technologies found in connected vehicles.

flip for answer

Answer

Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and cellular (4G/5G). V2X and the key fob RF also count.

flip back

Q-03

What does OTA stand for, and what does it allow a manufacturer to do?

flip for answer

Answer

Over-the-Air. It allows a manufacturer to deliver software updates to the vehicle remotely, without a physical connection at a dealership.

flip back

Q-04

Why does increased connectivity increase the cybersecurity attack surface of a vehicle?

flip for answer

Answer

Every communication channel is a potential entry point. A vehicle that communicates over cellular, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and V2X simultaneously has multiple interfaces that did not exist in purely mechanical vehicles — each one a path through which malicious input could be introduced.
